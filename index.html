Why Donate to Vanderbilt Ballet Theater?

Donating any amount helps Vanderbilt Ballet Theater continue to support our members and keep our club accessible to as many people as possible. Team funds are used to pay for costumes, props, and show production costs. Your donation helps us continue to provide a space for students to express themselves through dance and build a community of support and friendship.
